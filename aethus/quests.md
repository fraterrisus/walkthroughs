## Access Denied

*Trigger:* Find the sun shade near the big crater on the surface and the code-locked crate inside.

*Goal:* Open the crate using the code from the nearby datapad (0155)

## Arrival

*Trigger:* Start the actual game (not the Prologue).

*Goals:*

1. Continue along the canyon to the clearing.
2. Clear and collect some Metal Scrap.
3. Build a Makeshift Forge.
4. Build a Makeshift Habitat.
5. Construct a Bed.

## Deep Impact

*Trigger:* Find the elevator beyond Facility Alpha and examine the view.

*Goals:*

1. Construct a Trade Node; earn some credits; purchase the Oxygen Filters I upgrade.
2. Buy some more upgrades as needed, then enter the Meteorite Crater.
3. Explore the Crater and enter Delta (the "Crater Facility")
4. Explore Delta.
5. Find the Transmitter Facility behind Delta, and what happened to Anthony Shin.
6. Given Shin and Jackson's dead bodies, search the transmitter for more clues.
7. Release the lockdown using Shin's keycard and the Security Console.
8. Craft an EMP at the Electronics Workbench.
9. Drop the EMP into the Blast Door, then return to the surface.
10. Return to Delta and reactivate the Climber.

## Electric Dreams

*Trigger:* Return from the Lost World... with something.

*Goals:* Bring three Microelectronics to the Trade Node.

*Reward:* The blueprint for the Suit Charger.

## First Bite

*Trigger:* Get hungry.

*Goal:* Collect and eat a Cactus Fruit.

## Frontier Cooking

*Trigger:* Complete [Arrival](#arrival).

*Goal:* Eat a Grilled Cactus Fruit.

First, build a Scrap Grill. Then use the crafting interface to produce a Grilled Cactus Fruit, add it to your inventory, and eat it.

## Further Facilities

*Trigger*: Complete [Sick of Scrap](#sick-of-scrap).

*Goal:* Build a Stone Crusher and a Basic Smelter, and produce your first Kalynite Bar.

## Glint of Gold

*Trigger:* Find the datapad on the Storage Container *outside* Delta.

*Goal:* Find the gold-drilling site up on the hill behind Delta.

## Going Down

*Trigger:* Find the elevator beyond Facility Alpha and examine the view.

*Goal:* Find or craft a Power Cell and repair the elevator.

## Going Underground

*Trigger:* Complete [Lay of the Land](#lay-of-the-land).

*Goals:*

1. Build an RTG in your Habitat. The crafting ingredient you need (also called an RTG) is in a chest just inside the entrance to the Outer Caverns.

2. Explore the rest of the Outer Caves until you find the rockfall at the back of the Outer Caves.

3. Smelt a Kalynite Bar, construct a Crafting Bench, then make Mining Explosives and use them to clear the rockfall.

## Heavy Metal

*Trigger:* "Complete" [Working for the Man](#working-for-the-man), but really it seems to just be "return from your next mining run".

*Goal:* Deliver three Kalynite Frames to the Trade Node.

*Reward:* $2000

## Into the Darkness

*Trigger:* Complete [Deep Impact](#deep-impact) by reaching Epsilon.

*Goals:*

1. Locate the generator controls and restore power.
2. Find the Control Room.
3. Exit Epsilon via the Airlock off the Climber Room.
4. Locate the exterior generator in the courtyard and restore power.
5. Find Facility Kappa.
6. Investigate the Kappa Dam, and eventually drain it.

## Lay of the Land

*Trigger:* Complete [Arrival](#arrival).

*Goal:* Conduct a Suit Scan at the four points of interest on the surface: the Oasis, Crater's Edge, the Bloodfall, and the Cave Entrance.

## Leave Via the Back Door

*Trigger:* Complete [Glint of Gold](#glint-of-gold) by finding the Gold Drill behind Delta, and read the log files inside.

*Goal:* Find the "back door" exit from the caves that brings you out by the Bloodfall.

## Mechanic Required

*Trigger:* Try the door at Beta, and discover that the servos are fried.

*Goal:* Build an Electronics Workbench, then craft a Servomotor and use it to repair the door.

## The Menagerie

*Trigger:* Return from a mining run after completing [Heavy Metal](#heavy-metal).

*Goal:* Deliver ten [Mysterious Eggs](items/#mysterious-eggs) to the Trade Node.

*Reward:* $3000

## Moisture Farmer

*Trigger:* Find the blueprint for the Dew Catcher under the sun shade near the big crater on the surface.

*Goal:* Build a Dew Catcher.

## Out of This World

*Trigger:* Bring Meteorite back to your Outpost.

*Goal:* Deliver 15 Meteorite and 30 Meteor Glass to the Trade Node.

*Reward:* $3500

## Power Up

*Trigger:* Build an Access Platform and attach it to a Basic Habitat (part of [Going Underground](#going-underground))

*Goal:* Enter your Basic Habitat and build an RTG there.

## Road to Riches

*Trigger:* Complete [Going Underground](#going-underground).

*Goals:*

1. Construct a Trade Node.
2. Construct a Comms Antenna.
3. Purchase the "Mining Laser Crystal 1" upgrade.
4. Construct a Drone Pod.

## Sick of Scrap

*Trigger:* Build an Access Platform and attach it to a Basic Habitat (part of [Going Underground](#going-underground))

*Goal:* Construct a Regolith Printer in your Habitat. (Requires completing [Power Up](#power-up).)

## Skeletons of the Past

*Trigger:* Find the datapad from Caleb Vihaan in the courtyard of Epsilon.

*Goals:*

1. Investigate Lambda.
2. Get into the Secure Storage facility of Epsilon.

## Storage Wars

*Trigger:* Complete [Arrival](#arrival).

*Goal:* Build a Storage Crate.

## Strike the Earth

*Trigger:* Find the drill behind Beta.

*Goal:* Insert the appropriate Drill Bit into one of the drills you've found.

## Taking Shortcuts

*Trigger:* Interact with the Epsilon command terminal in Facility Control.

*Goals:*

1. Use a Bypass Mole to interact with the Facility Control terminal and download the blueprint for a Crater Climber.
2. Build the Climber Station on the surface to allow the Climber to connect all the way through.

## The Way Opens

*Trigger:* Complete [Going Underground](#going-underground).

*Goal*: Explore Habitat Alpha, located just beyond the cave-in.

## Working for the Man

*Trigger:* Comes up when you return to base, possibly for the first time after discovering Beta?

*Goal:* In theory, reach Level Two of the RAPP by buying things from the Market. Except Trudy seems to just start offering you quests the next time you return from a mining run, even if you haven't reached RAPP-2 yet.
