# Mining

## Regions:

- **Outside**, where you build your Outpost
- **Outer Caves**, the first cave entrance
- **Inner Caves**, the other side of the rockfall / the secret cave entrance. Includes **Alpha**, **Beta**, and the swampy ramp.
- **Outer Crater**, a step up in oxygen usage; the far end of the rock bridge past Beta and/or the waterfall region
- **Delta** or the Inner Crater, meteorite city.
